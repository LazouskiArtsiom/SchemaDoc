Features

Everything you need to understand your schema

No configuration, no cloud, no data ever leaves your machine. Just run and connect.

⬡

ER Diagrams

Auto-generated entity-relationship diagrams with column types, PK/FK annotations, and relationship arrows labeled with column-to-column info.

↓

PDF Export

Export your full schema documentation as a PDF — perfect for sharing with teammates or archiving snapshots of your database structure.

⇄

Schema Diff

Compare two snapshots side by side. Instantly see which tables and columns were added, removed, or modified between versions.

⚡

Instant switching

Keep multiple connections active simultaneously. Switch between databases in one click — schemas are cached locally for zero-latency switching.

🔒

Encrypted storage

Connection strings are encrypted with Windows DPAPI and stored locally. Nothing is ever sent to any server or third party.

◑

Dark & light mode

Fully theme-aware UI with persistent preference. Diagrams re-render automatically when you switch modes.

How it works

Up and running in 60 seconds

No installation wizard, no account, no setup.

1

Download & run

Download the zip, extract it, and double-click SchemaDoc.Web.exe. Your browser opens automatically.

2

Add a connection

Paste your connection string for SQL Server, PostgreSQL, MySQL, or Cosmos DB. It's stored encrypted on your machine.

3

Explore

Browse tables, view columns and row counts, generate ER diagrams, export PDFs, and compare schema versions.
